About Me


I'm a product marketer with 8+ years of experience in the e-commerce and cybersecurity spheres.


I've helped companies design, build, and launch online sales platforms both in-house and on Amazon.

From market research and product scoping to manufacturing contracting and export logistics, I bring extensive practical experience across the value chain.

My Book


The Internet: A Practical Guide for Beginners

As a non-technical undergraduate, I remember feeling under-equipped when I took my first job in digital consulting. After 8+ years in tech, I've written this book to help non-technical readers get to grips with the modern Internet landscape.


Following a brief history of the Internet, you’ll learn the basics of computer networking before diving into major topics like security, blockchain, and the Internet of Things. Particular focus is given to contemporary technologies like 5G, Wi-Fi 6, and decentralized computing.


A perfect first book for beginners and a succinct reference for professionals, this is a no-nonsense, “nothing you don’t need” guide to the Internet as it is today.
